## PCB Layout Critical Notes

| Component | Tab pad net | Action required |
|---|---|---|
| LT5560 ×3 (U501, U701, U1001) | **PGND** | Connect EP to GND plane — mandatory |
| AMS1084CM-5.0 (U102) | **+5V_P_AMP** | Isolated polygon on +5V_P_AMP net |
| AMS1117-3.3 (U101) | **+3.3 V** | Isolated polygon on +3.3 V net |
| AMS1117-5.0 (U104) | **+5 V** | Isolated polygon on +5 V net |
| BGA2869 (U401, U801) | No tab pad | Standard SOT-363 — GND pins only |
| PAM8406 (U1201) | No tab pad | Standard SOIC-16 |
